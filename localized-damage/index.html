Localized damage

by Subleader

  • Description
  • Configs
  • License

Damage are different depending on which limb is being hurt.

Servers can customize usage of this plugin with these settings

Arms Scale Damage (default: 0.4)

How much should arms damage be scaled by?

Head Scale Damage (default: 3)

How much should head damage be scaled by?

Leg Break Duration (default: 10)

Set the time that a leg remains broken.

Legs Scale Damage (default: 0.5)

How much should legs damage be scaled by?

Localized Damage (default: true)

Activate the Localized Damage.

Stomach Scale Damage (default: 0.8)

How much should stomach damage be scaled by?
